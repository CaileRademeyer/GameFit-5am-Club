About GameFit 5am Club

GameFit 5am Club is a mobile training squad delivering expert rugby, netball and hockey coaching across the region. We train players from under 7s to under 18s, with emphasis on skill mastery, fun, safety and measurable progress.

Rugby: We focus on tackle safety, set-piece technique and tactical awareness. Our coaching develops players from foundational skills to competitive performance.

Netball: We provide sport-specific conditioning for court movement, passing accuracy and defence. Our training builds footwork, spatial awareness and match-ready resilience.

Hockey: Our programmes develop stick skills, penalty corner execution and game-specific fitness. We enhance field awareness and rapid transitions for all ability levels.

1-on-1 Personal Training: Tailored sessions focused exclusively on individual athlete development. Our coaches provide personalised feedback, technique refinement and goal-specific progression to accelerate performance gains.

Sample Drills

  • Agility Ladder: Improve footwork and reaction time with dynamic ladder sequences.
  • Conditioning Circuits: Rotate through strength, cardio and plyometric stations for total-body fitness.
  • Tackle Technique: Safe and effective contact drills to build confidence in defence.
  • Team Relay Challenges: Boost teamwork and competitive spirit with relay-style drills.
  • Rugby Ball-Handling Grid: Increase passing precision and decision speed in small-sided possession games.
  • Netball Transition Sprint: Practice rapid change-of-direction and court awareness while carrying the ball through defensive lines.
  • Hockey Corner Set-Up: Simulate penalty corner routines with quick push passes, drag flick practice, and defensive pressure recovery.
  • Endurance Shuttle Series: Sport-specific shuttle sprints tailored for rugby, netball and hockey movement patterns and recovery demands.
